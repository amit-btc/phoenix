Features

Fully Audited Smart Contract

Our contract has been audited by the team at FTP antibot. From inception we found it necessary and vital to have a smart contract that is ready for deployment, rather then waiting until capital is obtained.

Liquidity Locked Forever

Phoenix tokens have been locked into uniswap ensuring an ample supply of tokens can be purchased and accessed as the community evolves and grows.

Anti-Bot

Their powerful contract development means that Phoenix token is secure and safe from manipulation by trading bots. As the marketplace and community continue to develop, we wanted to be sure that the assets acquired could not lose value due to legions of trading bots manipulating pricing.

NFT Marketplace

Our NFT marketplace will be designed to bring quality artists to the forefront while also providing a space for newer artists to flourish. For the time being we will require all potential artists to be vetted and go through an application process. The overall supply of NFTs might be lower, but the goal is to keep quality high.
